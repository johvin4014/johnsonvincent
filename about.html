About & Experience

My journey from U.S. Navy systems administrator to cybersecurity specialist — and why my hands‑on skills, leadership background and clearance make me a strong fit for your organization.

My Journey

I began my career in the U.S. Navy, where I spent over nine years administering databases and mission‑critical systems for aviation maintenance commands. During this time I developed a passion for cybersecurity, ensuring confidentiality, integrity and availability of sensitive data across globally deployed networks.

After separating from the military, I completed a Bachelor of Science in Information & Cybersecurity Operations (May 2026) and earned my CompTIA Security+ certification (IAT Level II). I built a fully documented SOC home lab using Wazuh SIEM, simulated multiple attack scenarios, and created Python tools to detect and respond to threats.

  • 2015–2024 – U.S. Navy Database & Systems Administrator (NALCOMIS) — ensured 99.9% availability of mission‑critical systems, mentored junior sailors and maintained a Secret clearance.
  • 2024–2026 – Completed B.S. in Cybersecurity at ECPI University, built home SOC lab, published case studies and competed in National Cyber League tournaments.
  • Present – Security Officer II at Children’s Hospital of The King’s Daughters — implement physical and logical security controls, investigate incidents and train staff on policies.
